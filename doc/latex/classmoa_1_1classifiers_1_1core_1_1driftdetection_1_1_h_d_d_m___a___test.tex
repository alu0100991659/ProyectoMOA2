\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Online drift detection method based on Hoeffding\textquotesingle{}s bounds. H\+D\+DM\textsubscript{{\itshape A}-\/test} uses the average as estimator. It receives as input a stream of real values and returns the estimated status of the stream\+: S\+T\+A\+B\+LE, W\+A\+R\+N\+I\+NG or D\+R\+I\+FT.

I. Frias-\/\+Blanco, J. del Campo-\/\+Avila, G. Ramos-\/\+Jimenez, R. Morales-\/\+Bueno, A. Ortiz-\/\+Diaz, and Y. Caballero-\/\+Mota, Online and non-\/parametric drift detection methods based on Hoeffding\textquotesingle{}s bound, I\+E\+EE Transactions on Knowledge and Data Engineering, 2014. D\+OI 10.\+1109/\+T\+K\+DE.2014.\+2345382.

Parameters\+:


\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item -\/d \+: Confidence to the drift
\item -\/w \+: Confidence to the warning
\item -\/t \+: Option to monitor error increments and decrements (two-\/sided) or only increments (one-\/sided) 
\end{DoxyItemize}

\begin{DoxyAuthor}{Author}
Isvani Frias-\/\+Blanco (\href{mailto:ifriasb@udg.co.cu}{\texttt{ ifriasb@udg.\+co.\+cu}}) 
\end{DoxyAuthor}
