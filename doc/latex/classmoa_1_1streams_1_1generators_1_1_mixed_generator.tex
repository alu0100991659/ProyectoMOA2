\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Abrupt concept drift, boolean noise-\/free examples. Four relevant attributes, two boolean attributes v,w and two numeric attributes from \mbox{[}0; 1\mbox{]}. The examples are classified positive if two of three conditions are satisfied\+: v,w, y $<$ 0,5 + 0,3 sin(3 $\ast$ P\+I $\ast$ x). After each context change the classification is reversed. Proposed by \char`\"{}\+Gama, Joao, et al. \char`\"{}Learning with drift detection.\char`\"{} Advances in artificial intelligence–\+S\+B\+I\+A 2004. Springer 
\+Berlin Heidelberg, 2004. 286-\/295.\char`\"{}
