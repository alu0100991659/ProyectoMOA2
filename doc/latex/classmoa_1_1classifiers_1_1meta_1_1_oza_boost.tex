\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Incremental on-\/line boosting of Oza and Russell.

See details in\+:~\newline
 N. Oza and S. Russell. Online bagging and boosting. In Artiﬁcial Intelligence and Statistics 2001, pages 105–112. Morgan Kaufmann, 2001.

For the boosting method, Oza and Russell note that the weighting procedure of Ada\+Boost actually divides the total example weight into two halves – half of the weight is assigned to the correctly classiﬁed examples, and the other half goes to the misclassiﬁed examples. They use the Poisson distribution for deciding the random probability that an example is used for training, only this time the parameter changes according to the boosting weight of the example as it is passed through each model in sequence.
