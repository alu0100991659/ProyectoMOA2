\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Ensemble of classifiers-\/based approach for incremental learning of concept drift, characterized by nonstationary environments (N\+S\+Es), where the underlying data distributions change over time. It learns from consecutive batches of data that experience constant or variable rate of drift, addition or deletion of concept classes, as well as cyclical drift.

Based on\+: Ryan Elwell and Robi Polikar. Incremental learning of concept drift in non-\/stationary environments. I\+E\+EE Transactions on Neural Networks, 22(10)\+:1517-\/1531, October 2011. I\+S\+SN 1045-\/9227. U\+RL \href{http://dx.doi.org/10.1109/TNN.2011.2160459}{\texttt{ http\+://dx.\+doi.\+org/10.\+1109/\+T\+N\+N.\+2011.\+2160459}}
