Detailed Description

The revised version of the Accuracy Updated Ensemble as proposed by Brzezinski and Stefanowski in "Reacting to Different Types of Concept Drift: The Accuracy Updated Ensemble Algorithm", IEEE Trans. Neural Netw, 2013.
